Favorite Apps By Cyril

These are my hand picked favorite iOS applications

Prioto - By Kha N.

Prioto combats issues with work shared by students and employees everywhere - over scheduling and burnout - by integrating two highly-regarded productivity techniques - the Eisenhower Method and Pomodoro Technique.

Downlaod for iOS

Augen Auf - By Alex M.

Pressured by time, bizarre monsters approaching, draw symbols to eliminate these creature and continue through a mysterious forest. My game Eyes Open recognizes player gesture that activate spells to erase monsters.

Download for iOS

Surreality - By Andrew Z.

**WINNER OF MAKE SCHOOL'S AUDIENCE CHOICE AWARD**

Surreality is a minimalistic physics puzzle game. Your goal? Utilize all tools given on screen to destroy the remaining blocks. The game is simple yet complicated, easy to pick up yet challenging later on. Give it a try!

Download for iOS

Silver Flight - A Game By Wes

Get ready to embark on an exciting flight through a mystical world, and encounter strange geometric beings

Download for iOS

NekoIsland - By Emily Y.

Neko Island is an addicting, simple but infinitely challenging game where the user is in control of a cat that can be moved around to balance an island. However, rocks constantly fall from the sky, hindering you from maintaining balance and staying alive.

Download for iOS

PhysRunner - By Rahul B.

PhysRunner is a puzzle-platformer that tests your reaction time, your skills at pattern-matching, your awareness of your surroundings, and your understanding of basic physics concepts like push and pull forces and gravity.

Download for iOS
