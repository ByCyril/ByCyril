About the App

FileStorks is a file sharing system where users can share and receive photos, documents, and more with others without the hardware, software, or physical limitations.

Design Philosophy

The design is meant to be minimal in content and focuse on its purpose.

Technology

FileStorks is designed with HTML, CSS, Javascript, BootStrap and JQuery. It also uses Firebase that supports both backend and hosting.

Third-Party Libraries

BootStrap for the design and JQuery for convinient interaction with the DOM.

Future Plans

FileStorks will eventually make its way to the app store to compliment both the web version and to take advantage of the iOS experience with it.
